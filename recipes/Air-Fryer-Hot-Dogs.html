Air Fryer Hot Dogs

Description

This recipe teaches you how to make hotdogs quickly and simply.

Ingredients

  • 4 hotdogs
  • 4 hotdog buns

Directions

  1. Preheat an air fryer to 400 degrees farenheit.
  2. Place buns in a single layer in the air fyer basket. Cook in air fryer for about 2 minutes (or until crisp). Remove buns
  3. Place hotdogs in a single layer in the air fryer basket. Cook for 3 minutes. Serve hotdogs in buns
